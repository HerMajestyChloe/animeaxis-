The Devil is A Part Timer Season 2 airs Thursday, July 14th

Chloe Nelson 07/07/2022

The first season of The Devil Is a Part-Timer ended 9 years ago last week. It’s crazy how fast time flies. What’s even crazier though is the fact a second season will be airing this week! This news took the anime community by shock a few months ago because no one expected a second season to be made in the first place. The anime will be receiving the simulcast treatment from Crunchyroll and should be released roughly an hour after Japan’s airing at 11:30 p.m. If you haven’t seen the first season, there is no time like the present. With only 13 episodes in season 1, it won’t take long to catch up with the new season.
